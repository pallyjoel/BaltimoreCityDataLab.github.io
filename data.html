Data Resources

A primary objective of the BCDL is to generate and promote data relevant to the challenges that face Baltimore City. This page will provide access to data produced or curated by BCDL, as well as information about other available data resources that are consistent with BCDL's objectives.

Baltimore Neighborhood Indicators Alliance The Baltimore Neighborhood Indicators Alliance-Jacob France Institute at the University of Baltimore (known as BNIA-JFI) is a nonprofit organization whose core mission is to provide open access to meaningful, reliable, and actionable data about, and for, the City of Baltimore and its communities. BNIA-JFI builds on and coordinates the related work of citywide nonprofit organizations, city and state government agencies, neighborhoods, foundations, businesses, and universities to support and strengthen the principle and practice of well informed decision making for change toward strong neighborhoods, improved quality of life, and a thriving city. JHU Center for a Livable Future The Center for a Livable Future aims to promotes research, development, and communication of information about the complex interrelationships among diet, food production, environment, and human health. It operates the Maryland Food System Map, which includes a comprehensive colleciton of datasets on how food is produced, distributed, and consumed in the state of Maryland. D F IP IP B
MD Report Card The State of Maryland Department of Education releases an annual Maryland Report Card that includes information on school performance and demographics for all schools in the Maryland State Education System. E D IP
Open Data Portal (MD) Maryland's open data projectis the central location for the state's publicly available data. Its goal is to increase transparency and accountability by providing residents with access to government data. E T D F T PS IP H B
Open Baltimore A data collection, consolidation, and distribution effort by the City of Baltimore and the Mayor's Office of Information Technology, the goal of Open Baltimore is to provide access to city data to support government transparency, openness, and innovative uses to help improve the lives of Baltimore residents, visitors, and businesses.
